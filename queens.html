These are my top choices for favorite queens out of all the RuPaul's Drag Race Seasons. This isnt counting the additional franchise shows like All Stars, UK, Netherlands, Thailand etc just to narrow it down a bit. I've seen every season at least three times at this point the drama is GREAT and the creativity is absolutely amazing. There really is only a handful of queens I could say I dont like, so narrowing them down to nine of my favourites is difficult.

Nina Flowers
Seasons:

RPDR S1, RPDR All Stars S1

Super cool, loved her unique style. Really funky and artsy. Very down to earth kind of personality. 10/10!

Adore Delano
Seasons: RPDR S6, RPDR All Stars S2

Had a totally different style, just did her own thing. Super talented singer. Too bad she left the season early I think she would've done well to stick it out. 8/10!

Alaska
Seasons: RPDR S5, RPDR All Stars S2

Just a hilarious queen, really liked her sense of humour. Struggled a bit in her first season personality wise probably due to some personal stuff going on but completely slayed it in All Stars. 8/10!

Alexis Mateo
Seasons: RPDR S3, RPDR All Stars S3

Definately more of a pageant queen which normally isn't my fav, but she's so funny and is a genuinly nice person. Not to say she can't be a bitch too but hey its TV. 9/10!

JuJuBee
Seasons: RPDR S3, RPDR All Stars S1 & S5, RPDR UK VS The World

Very well rounded queen, always looks great, extremely talented singer. Fell in love with her sense of humour and personality in general. Also she's a RuPaul dinosaur shes made 4 appearances. 9/10!

Katya Zamolodchikova
Seasons: RPDR S7, RPDR All Stars S2

Another queen with a really different kind of style, also extremely funny. I did like her more in All Stars than in her original season but she still performed very well. 7/10!

Kim Chi
Seasons: RPDR S8

Very humble, very cute, and an extremely talented make-up artist. Definately the dark horse in her season that really came through and suprised everyone. 10/10!

Latrice Royale
Seasons: RPDR S4, RPDR All Stars S1 & S4

Extremely professional, great looks, and hilarious too! Slays the house down boots every single time. Has also made 3 appearances on the show. 10/10!

Trixie Matell
Seasons: RPDR S7, RPDR All Stars S3

Had some really creative looks, and definately a funny queen. Made very visible improvements to her make-up and drag style throughout her season and on to All Stars 7/10!
